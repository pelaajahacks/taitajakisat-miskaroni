Design Document

The game focuses on speedrunning, and includes a timer to encourage the player to achieve the lowest time possible.

[Miska kertoo sit lisää kontrolleist ja mapist]
The controls are responsive

Level design is structured around distinct color-coded sections. Each color corresponds to different gameplay mechanics, helping communicate changes in mechanics, and supporting player readability.

To create time pressure, the player will die, if they are too slow. Behind them, is a wall that will kill you if it catches the player.

The game has a jump-pad, for the unique mechanic not listed on the clients basic movement plan.
